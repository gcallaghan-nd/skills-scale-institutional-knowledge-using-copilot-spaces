# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers shape user-facing experiences by researching user needs, designing intuitive interfaces, and collaborating with developers and product managers to deliver delightful, accessible products.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Collaborate with developers to implement design systems
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design practices
- Validate design decisions through user feedback and metrics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce friction and improve user satisfaction
- Maintain design consistency across product surfaces
- Ensure handoffs to development are clear and actionable

### Typical Communication
- Weekly design reviews with PM and engineering
- Design specs and style guides in shared documentation
- Usability test findings and user research reports
- Collaboration in sprint planning to ensure design readiness

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers orchestrate the end-to-end release process, coordinating between development, QA, product, and operations teams to ensure safe, predictable deployments with minimal disruption.

### Responsibilities
- Own the release calendar and deployment schedule
- Coordinate release readiness across dev, QA, and product teams
- Manage deployment checklists and go/no-go decisions
- Communicate release plans and status to stakeholders
- Facilitate post-deployment validation and rollback if needed
- Track release metrics and improve deployment processes

### Goals
- Deliver releases on schedule with high quality
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Maintain clear accountability and transparency in release process
- Drive continuous improvement in release velocity and reliability

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings with PM, dev leads, and QA
- Pre-deployment checklist reviews and go/no-go checkpoints
- Release notes and deployment announcements to stakeholders
- Post-deployment status updates and incident coordination
- Retrospectives to capture release-specific learnings

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts turn raw data into actionable insights, supporting data-driven decision-making across product, engineering, and business teams through analysis, visualization, and metric tracking.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Build dashboards and reports for project and product metrics
- Analyze user behavior and feature adoption
- Support A/B testing and experimentation
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in data
- Collaborate with PM and engineering on success criteria

### Goals
- Enable data-driven prioritization and decision-making
- Improve visibility into product health and user behavior
- Reduce time to insight through effective dashboards
- Ensure metric accuracy, consistency, and trustworthiness

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metric reviews with PM and stakeholders
- Dashboard walkthroughs and data deep-dives
- A/B test results and experiment readouts
- Metric definitions and data quality reports
- Integration with sprint planning to define success metrics

---

## Customer Support Liaison

### Role Summary
Customer Support Liaisons bridge the gap between customers and product teams, advocating for customer needs, escalating critical issues, and feeding user feedback into product decisions and retrospectives.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and escalate customer-reported incidents
- Document common issues and feature requests
- Provide product context and workarounds to support teams
- Feed customer feedback into retrospectives and planning
- Collaborate with PM on feature prioritization based on support trends
- Track support metrics (ticket volume, resolution time, sentiment)

### Goals
- Improve customer satisfaction and reduce support burden
- Ensure customer voices influence product roadmap
- Accelerate incident resolution through clear communication
- Build knowledge bases and self-service resources

### Typical Communication
- Daily incident escalation and triage with dev/ops teams
- Weekly support metrics and trend reports to PM and leadership
- Customer feedback summaries in sprint retrospectives
- Feature request documentation and prioritization input
- Collaboration on release notes and user-facing communications
